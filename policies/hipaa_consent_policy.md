# Internal Patient Data & Consent Policy
Policy ID: IP-PRIV-039 | Effective: 2026 | Governs: HIPAA §164.508 compliance

## Section 2.1 — Required Consent Elements
Valid patient consent requires: patient full name, date of birth, dated
signature, and explicit description of what data will be used/disclosed
and to whom.

## Section 2.2 — Emergency Contact Requirements
Intake forms must capture: emergency contact name, relationship, and at
least one verified phone number. Missing emergency contact info blocks
intake completion per internal safety policy IP-ADM-017.

## Section 2.3 — Revocation Rights
All consent forms must include clear instructions for how a patient may
revoke consent, per HIPAA's right-to-revoke provision. Absence of
revocation instructions renders consent invalid.

## Section 2.4 — Minimum Necessary Standard
Any data collection must be limited to the minimum necessary for the
stated treatment/payment/operations purpose (HIPAA §164.502(b)).
