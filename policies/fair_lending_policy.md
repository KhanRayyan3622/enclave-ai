# Internal Fair Lending & Underwriting Policy
Policy ID: IP-ADM-042 | Effective: 2026

## Section 3.1 — Debt-to-Income (DTI) Thresholds
Standard conventional loans require a back-end DTI ratio not exceeding 43%.
Applications with DTI between 43-45% require documented compensating factors
(reserves ≥ 6 months, LTV ≤ 75%, or credit score ≥ 740) and second-underwriter
sign-off per Section 3.4.

## Section 3.2 — Loan-to-Value (LTV) Requirements
Conventional loans without mortgage insurance require LTV ≤ 80% (i.e., minimum
20% down payment). LTV between 80-95% requires PMI per Section 5.2.

## Section 3.3 — Credit Score Minimums
Minimum credit score for standard approval: 620. Scores 620-679 require
manual underwriting review. Scores ≥ 680 qualify for automated approval path
subject to DTI/LTV requirements above.

## Section 3.4 — Fair Lending / ECOA Compliance
Per the Equal Credit Opportunity Act, all applications must be evaluated using
identical criteria regardless of race, color, religion, national origin, sex,
marital status, or age. Any DTI or LTV exception must be logged in the
override tracker and reviewed quarterly for disparate impact patterns.

## Section 3.5 — Required Application Fields
A complete application must include: verified income, requested loan amount,
full monthly debt obligations, property value/LTV, credit score, employment
history (minimum 2 years or documented exception), loan purpose, and voluntary
demographic data (for fair-lending monitoring only, never used in approval).
