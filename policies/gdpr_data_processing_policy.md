# Internal Data Processing & Vendor Policy
Policy ID: IP-LEGAL-011 | Effective: 2026 | Governs: GDPR Art. 5, 6, 28

## Section 4.1 — Storage Limitation
Per GDPR Article 5(1)(e), personal data must not be stored longer than
necessary for the purpose it was collected. Indefinite retention clauses
in vendor contracts are non-compliant and must be flagged.

## Section 4.2 — Processor Obligations
Any vendor/processor handling personal data on our behalf must be bound
by a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) per Article 28, specifying purpose,
duration, and security measures.

## Section 4.3 — Data Residency
Where a data-sovereignty mandate applies, personal data must not be
transferred outside the approved jurisdiction without an adequacy
decision or approved safeguard (Article 44-49).
