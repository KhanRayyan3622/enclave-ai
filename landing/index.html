The Problem
Regulated data can't take the trip to the cloud.
Compliance Violation

Sending patient records, financial filings, or loan applications to a third-party API is often a violation before the first token is generated — GDPR, HIPAA, and sovereign-AI mandates require data to stay in a controlled environment.

The Old Tradeoff

Most "on-prem AI" runs one small model on one small GPU — a fraction of what cloud-based agentic AI can do. Real compliance work needs a full multi-agent pipeline, not a compromise.
